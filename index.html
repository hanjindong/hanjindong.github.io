Jindong Han

 

I am currently a Ph.D. student (since Fall 2021) at the Academy of Interdisciplinary Studies, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), advised by Prof. Hao Liu and Prof. Hui Xiong. My general research areas include data mining and machine learning, with a particular focus on spatio-temporal data mining, urban computing, and AI for science. My long-term goal is to develop capable spatio-temporal intelligence systems for modeling our ever-changing world, solving challenging societal and scientific problems.

E-mail: jhanao@connect.ust.hk

Research Interest

My current research interests lie primarily in machine learning for large-scale spatio-temporal data, relevant topics include:

  • Spatio-Temporal Data Mining (Large Urban Models, Time Series Analysis, etc)

  • AI for Science (AI4Climate, Scientific AI Agents, etc)
